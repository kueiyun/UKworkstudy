What is YMS?

The Youth Mobility Scheme (YMS) allows young people from countries like Taiwan, Japan, South Korea, and more to work and study in the UK for up to 2 years.

This programme combines 4-16 weeks of English study (B2 level required) with a paid placement in hospitality (e.g. Marriott, Sheraton, Hilton).

Placement roles include: Reception, Housekeeping, Kitchen Porter, F&B, Marketing, Events, and more.

Steps to Join

  1. Apply for the YMS visa
  2. Reach B2 level English (minimum 4 weeks study at Malvern House)
  3. Submit CV and do interview
  4. Get matched to paid work placement (min. 6 months)
  5. Gain UK work experience and improve your employability
